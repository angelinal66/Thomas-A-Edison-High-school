Who are we?

Thomas A. Edison is a unique school that offers strong academic and career and technical education programs.We provide a real college and career ready institute. The mission of Thomas A. Edison Career and Technical Education High School is to develop leaders of tomorrow by preparing all students to meet the high academic, technical, civic and workforce challenges of the twenty-first century.We believe that strong leadership throughout our united community of faculty members, students, parents, and associates will move us towards the following goals: academic rigor, high standards for assessing students' performances and continuous school improvement. The vision of our school is to prepare and motivate students by instilling in them critical reasoning skills, respect for themselves and one another and a commitment to excellence, kindness and perseverance.

When Can I Use The library?

The library is open periods 1-9. Visit during lunch or with a pass from your teacher. Students can combine lunch and a visit to the library. First, go to the lunchroom to eat and then wait for the library announcement – a faculty member will escort students.

How Can I Borrow Books?

You are allowed to check out two books at a time for two weeks. The due date is stamped in the back of the book. Please return books when they are due. Those who need extra time may renew their books.

What Can I Do In the Library?

The libary is a quiet safe place for our students. We welcome students to come if they would like to do assisnments or study as we have lots of textbooks availabe. We also offer tudoring and test prep. We encourage taking books out as well.

Parent Teacher Association

Parent-Teacher Association

The Parent-Teacher Association of Thomas Edison was established to promote the school's educational excellence and welfare. Since then, it has served as a conduit to highlight parental concerns to the administration. In addition, the association works closely with the Principal and acts as a communications system between parents and the school to exchange views in both curricular and non-curricular areas. Please visit this page often to read about all the great things we have planned, give us feedback, and share your opinions. Thank you for your continued support!

About the PTA The Thomas A. Edison High School Parent Teacher Association is a volunteer organization joining both parents and teachers in a shared venture: enhancing education to enrich our children's lives and learning experiences. This goal is accomplished in various ways, including sponsoring specific programs for students and parents, sharing parenting support, providing information to parents about the school and activities, and creating opportunities for administration, teachers, parents, and students to collaborate. All student's families are automatically members of the PTA, although your voluntary contribution would help support our activities. We encourage your active participation and support, as we look forward to seeing you at our meetings and events. The PTA is organized and constituted in accordance with the NYC Chancellor's Regulations A-660.
